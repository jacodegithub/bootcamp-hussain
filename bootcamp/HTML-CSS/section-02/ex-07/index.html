Take a bow, Isro – Lunar landing is the most significant Indian scientific achievement. Let’s build on it

August 23, 2023, will be a red-letter day for not just Isro but all of Indian science. Chandrayaan-3 successfully landed on the south pole of the Moon, making India the first country to achieve this feat. It represents a successful culmination of the third attempt when Isro inserted a spacecraft into lunar orbit.
